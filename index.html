Introducing Nälij

Everyone possesses knowledge of potential value to someone else.

Powerful search engines can connect us with an overwhelming amount of information, but not necessarily the knowledge or expertise we need to attain our commercial goals. Because of transactional, technical and institutional barriers, collaborating and exchanging knowledge with others is one of the greatest challenges we face as a species. Yet, there is a vast ocean of expertise that awaits if only an efficient mechanism can be found to tap it.

knowledge share the blockchain way

Nälij is a decentralized marketplace for knowledge seekers and knowledge providers in business, academia, research, THE ARTS and government to collaborate across geographic, language and payment barriers.

Nälij leverages and combines the power of Blockchain technology, crowdsourcing and built-in user ratings to facilitate the dissemination of knowledge creating a diverse ecosystem where all participants – knowledge seekers, knowledge providers, translators and editors – win.

When knowledge seekers & providers have a
way to find and rate one another, and
transactional and language barriers are
removed, big outcomes are possible

  • Innovation accelerates.
  • Scientific and technology development is expedited.
  • Understanding across cultures is improved.
  • More and better quality content is driven to the web.
  • Opportunities for innovators and experts, big and small alike, especially in less developed and/or more restrictive economies, are increased.

While the business model of Nälij is
commercially grounded with a profit motive,
our social mission is to democratize access to
knowledge that has market value and help
drive the decentralized economy.

We aim to help people and businesses from across the world more easily transact with one another, increasing trade, investment and income opportunities.

And by facilitating cross border information and cultural exchanges, we increase global understanding – an important prerequisite for avoiding conflict.

Nälij Anatomy

Nalij Use Cases

Freelancers/Consultants

Nälij allows freelancers and consultants from across the globe to create personalized professional profiles, including their billing rates, making them discoverable by businesses seeking specific kinds of expertise and experience. Transacting in cryptocurrency overcomes payment barriers, particularly if the knowledge seekers and providers are located in different countries. Nälij’s built-in user ratings provide an inherent level of quality control.

Consultants can upload their conference presentations to the Nälij platform making them discoverable by the Nälij user community and enhancing their visibility for potential consulting engagements.

Nalij Use Cases

Journalistic/Opinion

A writer who publishes a weekly analysis of the cryptocurrency market provides Nalij with access to his newsletter archive. Nalij users can purchase individual newsletters, translated if necessary, through the Nalij platform.

A newspaper can place a Nalij button on their website allowing readers to purchase individual articles of interest with cryptocurrency and crowdsource translations if needed.

Nalij Use Cases

Education

Nalij provides teachers and other professionals as well as organizations with online educational content another channel to reach their target market, with user ratings providing enhanced web presence for the best content.

Nalij Use Cases

Cultural/Artistic

Nalij provides a direct way for authors, artists and musicians to sell their works directly to consumers, facilitating translations where appropriate, as well as payment for electronic or physical copies, as desired by the consumer.

Nalij Use Cases

Scientific/Technical

A university wants to generate income through the sale of university-sponsored research. By connecting their content database to Nalij, it becomes discoverable by the Nalij user community. Nalij facilitates translations, where needed, as well as payment.

A scientific journal can place a Nalij button on their website allowing readers to purchase individual articles of interest with cryptocurrency and crowdsource translations if needed
